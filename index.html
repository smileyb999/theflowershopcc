? FAQ

Got questions?

We're at 2803 Fort Worth Dr in Denton, tucked behind the gas station — look for the bright yellow truck. (Yes, the Dallas Observer wasn't kidding about the gas station part.)

They're roasted by Coffee Del Rey out of Plano, TX — small-batch and rotating. Origins change throughout the year (recently a single-origin from Papua New Guinea). Retail bags are usually available at the truck if you want to brew at home.

Yes — build your order on the menu page and we'll have it ready when you pull up. Online ordering runs during business hours.

Of course. Oat and almond milk are available as substitutions (+$1), and several syrups come sugar-free. Just ask at the window — we'll figure it out together.

We rotate specials every few months. Right now Spring Sips are live: Bluebonnet Nectar, Baklava Oat Shaken Espresso, and Banana Jam Latte. New drops always land on Instagram first.
