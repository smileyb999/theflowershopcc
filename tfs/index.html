04Behind the truck

Every part of it is personal.

The Flower Shop Coffee Co. started with Miranda and a love for drinks that feel a little special. The beans are carefully sourced from Coffee Del Rey. The stickers are free. The hand painted details on the windowsill come from her mom.

Nothing here is overly polished, and that’s on purpose. It’s meant to feel warm, welcoming, and worth coming back for.
